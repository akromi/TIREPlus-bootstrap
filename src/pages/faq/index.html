DriveON (formerly known as the Drive Clean program) is Ontario's vehicle safety inspection program. A safety inspection is required when transferring vehicle ownership, registering an out-of-province vehicle, or rebuilding a salvage vehicle. Tire Plus is a licensed DriveON Vehicle Inspection Centre. Inspections start at $199.
