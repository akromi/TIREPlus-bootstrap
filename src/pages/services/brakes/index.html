Signs you need brake service

  • Squealing or squeaking when braking — often the wear indicator on pads
  • Grinding noise — pads are worn through, metal on metal — stop driving and call us immediately
  • Vibration or pulsation through the brake pedal or steering wheel — usually warped rotors
  • Soft or spongy pedal — could indicate air or moisture in brake lines
  • Pulling to one side when braking
  • Brake warning light on dashboard
  • Longer stopping distance

Why brake fluid flush matters

Brake fluid absorbs moisture from the air over time. Moisture lowers the boiling point and can lead to spongy pedal feel or, in extreme cases, brake failure under heavy use. Manufacturers recommend flushing every 2 to 3 years. Most drivers never have it done — and most don't realize how much better the brakes feel afterward. We highly recommend this service.
