Check engine light diagnostics

The check engine light can mean dozens of different things — from a loose gas cap to a serious engine problem. Modern diagnostics involve more than just reading codes; the code only points toward a system, not a specific failure. We use professional scan tools and follow manufacturer diagnostic procedures to find the actual cause, not just guess.
