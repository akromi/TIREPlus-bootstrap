What is the DriveON program?

DriveON is the Government of Ontario's vehicle inspection program. The InspectiON program covers mandatory safety inspections for vehicles that need to pass a Safety Standards Certificate. Tire Plus is a licensed DriveON Vehicle Inspection Centre, authorized by the Ministry of Transportation to perform these inspections in Orleans.

When do you need a safety inspection?

  • Transferring vehicle ownership from one person to another (private sale)
  • Registering an out-of-province vehicle in Ontario
  • Rebuilt status vehicles — to remove the rebuilt status and re-register
  • Some commercial vehicles — annual safety inspections
  • Lapsed registration in some cases

You do not need a safety inspection for a routine renewal of your existing registration.
