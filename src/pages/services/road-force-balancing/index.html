What is Road Force balancing?

Standard wheel balancing only measures static and dynamic weight imbalance. It doesn't detect imperfections in the tire's roundness or stiffness — and those are the most common causes of vibrations that won't go away after a regular balance.

Hunter Road Force Elite balancers apply a load roller (~600 kg of force) to the tire as it spins, simulating the load of an actual road. This measures force variation — tiny inconsistencies in how the tire flexes under load — and tells us exactly how to position the tire on the wheel to minimize vibration.

When you need Road Force balancing

  • Steering wheel vibration at highway speeds (90 to 110 km/h) that doesn't go away after standard balancing
  • Just installed expensive tires and want them perfectly smooth
  • Premium, performance, or luxury vehicle where ride quality matters
  • Used or older tires you suspect have minor defects
  • Vibration returned soon after a regular balance
