Signs you need exhaust repair

  • Loud rumbling or roaring from underneath
  • Rattling or hanging noise over bumps
  • Hissing or popping sounds
  • Smell of exhaust fumes inside the cabin
  • Reduced fuel economy
  • Vibration through pedals or steering wheel
  • Visible rust holes or damaged hangers

What we fix

  • Muffler replacement
  • Pipe sections — flex pipes, mid-pipes, tail pipes
  • Hangers and clamps
  • Resonator replacement
  • Catalytic converter replacement (when required)
  • Welding repairs on cracked or rusted-through sections
  • Heat shield repair or replacement

Ottawa winters and exhaust systems

Salt and slush from Ontario winters are brutal on exhaust components. Most exhaust repairs we see are rust-related — pipes that thinned out over years of road salt exposure. We can often patch or replace just the affected section rather than the whole system, saving you money.

Catalytic converter theft

Cat converter theft is a growing problem in Ottawa. If your vehicle suddenly sounds like a race car when you start it, this is the most likely cause. We can replace the converter and recommend protective shields or marking that may deter future theft.
