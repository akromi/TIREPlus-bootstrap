About Tire Plus

Serving Orleans and east Ottawa drivers since 2004.

Tire Plus opened its doors at 2006 St. Joseph Blvd, Orleans in 2004. Over 20 years later, we're still at the same address, still answering the same phone number, and still doing the same thing: honest, dependable tire and auto service for the families and businesses of east Ottawa.

What started as a neighbourhood tire shop has grown into a full-service auto repair centre — tires, wheels, brakes, alignments, exhaust, A/C, suspension, batteries, and licensed DriveON safety inspections — without losing the small-shop feel our customers come back for.

Why customers choose Tire Plus

Honest assessments

If you don't need it, we won't sell it. Free brake and tire inspections, walk-in, with a clear yes-or-no on what actually needs to be done.

Transparent pricing

Tire prices include installation, balancing, and nitrogen fill. Alignments from $159, oil changes from $59, DriveON inspections from $199. The price you see is the price you pay.

Open Saturdays

One of the few Orleans auto shops open on Saturday (8 AM – 4 PM). Get the work done without missing a workday.

Professional equipment

Hunter precision alignment equipment, Road Force Elite balancing, and the certified diagnostic tools needed for a modern garage. Done right the first time.
